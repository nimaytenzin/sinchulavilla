Getting Here

Directions to Sinchula Villa

From Paro Airport

Our hotel is conveniently located just 15 minutes from Paro International Airport. We offer complimentary airport transfer service for all guests. Please inform us of your arrival details when making your reservation.

From Paro Town

Take the main road towards Thimphu for 2km, then turn right at the Sinchula Villa signboard. Follow the pine-lined road for 500m to reach our entrance.

Nearby Landmarks

  • Paro Dzong (Fortress) - 2km
  • National Museum - 2.5km
  • Tiger's Nest Monastery - 8km
  • Weekend Market - 2km
