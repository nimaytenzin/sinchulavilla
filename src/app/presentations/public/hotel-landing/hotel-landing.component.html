About Sinchula Villa

Nestled approximately 2 kilometers from Paro town in tranquil pine forests, Sinchula Villa offers sweeping views of the ancient Paro Dzong and the nearby museum.

Perfectly positioned amid fruit orchards and towering pines, our family-owned boutique hotel provides serenity without sacrificing access to Bhutan's most treasured cultural sites.

Experience the harmony of modern luxury and authentic Bhutanese architecture, where warm hospitality meets the timeless beauty of the Last Shangri-La.
